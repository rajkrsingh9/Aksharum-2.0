FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Everything you need to know before you sign up.

What exactly is School 2.0 and how is it different from other school software?

School 2.0 is a multi-tenant School ERP built for modern Indian schools. Unlike tools that focus on one area, School 2.0 connects attendance, results, exams, fees, communication, documents, and more into a single platform. Every role — admin, teacher, student, parent — sees exactly their world, enforced server-side. No switching between apps, no re-entering data.

Do I need to use all 13 modules, or can I start with just a few?

Absolutely not. School 2.0 is modular by design — you enable only what you need. Start with Attendance and Results. Add Fees and Exams when you're ready. Every module toggles on or off anytime with zero downtime. You pay only for what you actually use.

Is my school's data safe? Can other schools on the platform see it?

Never. School 2.0 uses a true multi-tenant architecture — every school runs in its own completely isolated environment. Your data, users, and configurations are private. There is zero possibility of cross-tenant access. Every API route validates the requesting user's school identity server-side before returning anything.

How do teachers, students, and parents get access to their accounts?

The School Admin creates accounts for teachers, students, and parents from the dashboard. Each person receives credentials and immediately sees their role-specific view. Teachers see their classes. Students see their own records. Parents see their child's data only. Access is enforced at every level.

How does the secure online exam system prevent cheating?

Teachers create timed MCQ exams. When a student starts, every tab switch, window blur, and focus loss is logged server-side in real time. The exam auto-submits when time runs out. Admins view full violation logs per student. Results and analytics are ready the moment the exam ends — no manual grading needed.

What does pricing look like and is there a free trial?

Pricing is modular and school-size based — you pay for the modules you enable with no minimum seat count. We offer a free live demo where our team walks through the platform with you. Book from the navigation bar. No credit card required upfront.

How long does it take to set up and go live?

Most schools are live within a single day. The School Admin signs up, creates the school structure — classes, sections, subjects — enables modules, and invites staff. No IT team required, no server setup, no installation. Everything runs in the browser.

What support is available after we onboard?

We provide dedicated onboarding support and ongoing help via email and in-platform messaging. Our team assists with initial setup, staff training, and technical questions. Most queries are resolved within a few hours. A comprehensive help documentation is also available inside the platform at all times.
