Why Join Us

Work that actually matters.

We're not building features for features' sake. Every line of code directly impacts a teacher's day, a student's future, a school's ability to run.

Real Impact, Real Fast

At Aksharum you're not a cog. Every feature you ship reaches hundreds of schools and thousands of students from day one. No layers, no waiting.

Small Team, Big Ownership

You'll own entire areas of the product — from architecture to design decisions. No managers approving your every PR. We trust the people we hire.

Modern Stack, Clean Codebase

Node.js, Express 5, MongoDB — a real codebase you'll actually enjoy working in. No legacy mess, no "we'll fix it later" culture.

Remote & Flexible

Work from wherever. Hours that fit your life. We care about what you build, not where you sit or what time your camera turns on.

Grow With Us

Early team members shape the culture, the codebase, and the company. The people joining now will be the leads, the architects, the decision-makers of tomorrow.

We Do Things Right

Security by architecture, not afterthought. Privacy built in from day one. Decisions documented. We're building something we're genuinely proud of.
